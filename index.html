Welcome

Thank you for your patience with us as we bring this site up to speed. The goal of ResourcesNow is to curate community resources that are available for people to use in all stages of their ServiceNow journey. Please bear with me as I try to get things updated, I will announce the official release of this site later tonight on twitter, the #community-bridging channel on SNDevs.com, LinkedIn, and anywhere else that I can reach.

If you want to contribute resources to be linked to here, please use this form. I will do my best to keep the site updated with new things as they are submitted, and I will work for a better automatic way for the new resources to be added as they are approved in the future.

Also please forgive the CSS formatting of the site. That is still very much WIP.
